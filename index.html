RealityOS is a decentralized, AI-assisted Reality Version Control System that transforms public claims — news events, policy announcements, corporate promises, scientific findings, and social narratives — into permanent, evolving evidence objects.

Unlike traditional fact-checking systems that issue static true/false verdicts and then move on, RealityOS continuously tracks how claims evolve over time. It links evidence, counter-evidence, revisions, predictions, outcomes, and competing interpretations into a transparent, auditable knowledge graph.

The platform functions as Git for Reality — a versioned, branched, collaborative system where citizens, researchers, journalists, institutions, governments, and organizations can collectively verify, challenge, update, and preserve public knowledge.

It creates a permanent societal memory layer where information cannot simply disappear, be rewritten, or be detached from its historical evidence trail. Every revision is preserved. Every branch is visible. Every outcome is measured.

RealityOS moves the fundamental question from "Is this true?" to "What evidence exists, how has it evolved, what outcomes occurred, and how confident should we be today?" — a shift that aligns information verification with how knowledge actually works.

The Problem

Why Information Systems Are Failing Us

The modern information ecosystem suffers from deep structural failures that static fact-checking cannot fix.

📡

Ephemeral Information

News articles get updated silently. Promises vanish. Context is stripped away. There is no institutional memory linking what was said to what actually happened.

⚖️

Binary Verdicts

True/False ratings collapse complex, evolving realities into single judgments. As evidence grows, the original verdict rarely gets updated — and when it does, the correction reaches far fewer people.

🔒

Authority Gatekeeping

Confidence in claims is determined by who controls the platform, not by the weight of evidence. Competing interpretations are suppressed rather than transparently maintained.

🧩

Fragmented Evidence

Evidence for any claim is scattered across hundreds of sources with no structured linkage. Connecting a promise to its outcome requires exhaustive manual research.

📉

Zero Outcome Tracking

Politicians make predictions. Companies publish forecasts. Researchers release findings. But there is no systematic mechanism to measure what actually happened against what was claimed.

🎭

Hidden Incentive Structures

Every major narrative has stakeholders with financial, political, and institutional interests. Current systems have no way to systematically surface who benefits from which version of events.

How It Works

Four Steps to Living Knowledge

From raw claim to permanent, measurable, branched evidence — automatically and transparently.

01

Ingest

Claims are captured from news, official statements, social media, scientific papers, and institutional publications — automatically tagged, timestamped, and signed.

02

Graph

Each claim is linked to related events, actors, evidence, counter-claims, and historical precedents in a growing knowledge graph with typed relationships.

03

Branch

When disagreements arise, competing interpretations fork into transparent branches. No perspective is deleted; each carries its own evidence and contributor history.

04

Measure

Predictions and promises are automatically scheduled for outcome verification. Actual results are linked back, confidence scores updated, and variance analyzed.

Key Features

A Complete Toolkit for
Truth Verification

Every component of RealityOS is purpose-built to overcome the failures of existing information infrastructure.

🧬

Living Evidence Objects

Every public statement becomes a permanent, structured object containing the original claim, supporting and contradicting evidence, source history, revision history, confidence metrics, contributor attributions, and outcome measurements. Nothing is lost; everything is linked.

🌿

Censorship-Resistant Evidence Branching

When disagreements occur, alternative interpretations fork into transparent evidence branches rather than being suppressed or deleted. Each branch carries its own evidence chain, contributor list, and confidence trajectory.

claim: "Policy X increased employment"
Branch A: Govt. Bureau Statistics
Branch B: Independent Economists
Branch C: Citizen Impact Surveys
Branch D: Academic Meta-Analysis
📈

Dynamic Confidence Engine

Confidence is never determined by authority or platform ownership. It emerges dynamically from evidence quality, source diversity, historical accuracy of contributors, verification status, peer review, and outcome consistency — continuously recalculated as new evidence arrives.

🕸️

Incentive Mapping

RealityOS automatically generates an Incentive Graph for every major narrative — identifying who benefits, who loses, what financial relationships exist, and what political or organizational pressures are at play. Makes hidden agendas structurally visible.

🎯

Outcome Accountability Layer

Every prediction, promise, and forecast becomes systematically measurable. RealityOS tracks scheduled checkpoints, expected vs. actual outcomes, and produces variance analysis automatically.

promise: "1 million jobs in 3 years"
Y1 target: 330k → Actual: 280k (−15%)
Y2 target: 660k → Actual: pending
Y3 target: 1M → Actual: scheduled
🌍

National & Global Memory Graph

A continuously expanding memory network linking policies, events, media reports, public statements, outcomes, and historical evidence into a queryable, navigable graph — designed to serve future generations as institutional memory that cannot be erased.

🤖

AI-Assisted Evidence Analysis

AI agents continuously monitor new publications, statements, and data releases — automatically linking new evidence to existing objects, flagging contradictions, suggesting related claims, and surfacing patterns across the knowledge graph that human analysts would miss.

🔐

Cryptographic Provenance

Every evidence submission, revision, and vote is cryptographically signed and timestamped. Edit history is immutable. Contributor identities are verifiable. The system cannot be silently tampered with — every change is on the permanent record.

🛡️

Selective Privacy Controls

Whistleblowers can submit evidence with source protection. Sensitive institutional records can be sealed with time-lock disclosure. The system balances radical transparency for outputs with appropriate confidentiality for vulnerable sources.

Use Cases

Real Applications
Across Every Domain

RealityOS is domain-agnostic. Every sector that relies on public claims, promises, or predictions benefits from a living evidence layer.

🏛️

Policy Promise Tracking

Every electoral promise and policy commitment becomes automatically trackable — with outcome checkpoints, variance reports, and a permanent record accessible to any citizen.

example "We will reduce inflation to 4% within 18 months" → tracked vs actual CPI data quarterly
🗳️

Electoral Claim Verification

During elections, candidate claims are linked to evidence in real time. Voters can see the full context, competing interpretations, and historical track record of each candidate.

example "My record on jobs" → linked to government data, independent analysis, and outcome measurements from prior term
📜

Legislative Impact Tracking

Bills and laws are linked to their stated intentions at passage, then tracked against measured outcomes — creating an auditable record of legislative efficacy.

example Infrastructure Bill 2023 → stated impact vs measured infrastructure improvement indices over 5 years
🌐

International Commitments

Treaty obligations, climate pledges, and international agreements are monitored against actual national statistics with multi-branch interpretations from participating nations.

example Net-zero pledges → tracked against national emissions data, verified by independent monitoring bodies
🔬

Replication Crisis Tracking

Scientific papers are linked to their replication attempts, corrections, retractions, and follow-up studies — giving an accurate, evolving picture of scientific consensus on any topic.

example Study X claims finding → linked to 12 replication attempts, 3 contradictions, 2 meta-analyses
💊

Clinical Claim Lifecycle

Pharmaceutical claims are tracked from initial trials through real-world outcomes, post-market surveillance data, and independent safety reviews.

example Drug X efficacy claim → Phase 3 trials, post-approval data, adverse event reports, independent reviews
🌡️

Climate Data Provenance

Climate claims are linked to underlying datasets, measurement methodologies, modeling assumptions, and competing interpretations from different research groups.

example Temperature record claim → raw data sources, adjustment methodology, multiple independent analyses
📊

Statistical Claim Auditing

Statistical findings are cross-linked to their underlying datasets, methodologies, funding sources, and alternative analytical approaches that may yield different conclusions.

example "Unemployment at X%" → methodology docs, alternative measures, historical comparators
💼

ESG Commitment Tracking

Corporate sustainability pledges, diversity targets, and ethical commitments are tracked against measurable outcomes — creating permanent accountability records visible to investors and the public.

example "Carbon neutral by 2030" → annual emissions data, methodology, third-party audits, progress tracking
📈

Earnings Guidance Verification

Forward guidance from company leadership is systematically tracked against reported financials — with incentive mapping showing stock-compensation relationships.

example "Q3 revenue $X–$Y guidance" → actual reported revenue, deviation analysis, management credibility score
🏭

Product Claim Accountability

Marketing claims about product capabilities, safety, or benefits are linked to independent testing, regulatory assessments, and consumer outcome data.

example "Clinically proven to reduce X by 40%" → study details, replication status, regulatory review
🤝

M&A and Partnership Claims

Deal rationales, synergy projections, and integration promises are tracked against post-merger performance — creating permanent accountability for executive decisions.

example Merger synergy claims → 3-year post-close performance vs projections, analyst assessments
📰

Story Update Tracking

News articles are linked to all subsequent updates, corrections, retractions, and contradicting coverage — giving readers a complete view of how understanding evolved.

example Breaking news item → 14 updates, 2 corrections, 3 major contradicting reports, final resolution
🎙️

Source Attribution Integrity

Anonymous source claims are tracked over time. When sources later reveal themselves or claims are confirmed/denied, the original article's evidence record is updated.

example "Sources say X" → later attribution, confirmation or denial, confidence trajectory
🔗

Cross-Publication Consistency

The same event across different publications is compared structurally — revealing where framing diverges, where selective emphasis occurs, and where outright contradiction exists.

example Same event → coverage across 24 publications, divergence analysis, selective emphasis mapping
🦠

Misinformation Lifecycle

False claims are tracked from origin through spread, correction attempts, persistence, and eventual resolution — building a comprehensive map of how misinformation propagates and dies.

example Viral false claim → origin, spread velocity, correction reach, persistence rate, final belief impact

Governance

Designed to Resist Capture

No single actor — government, corporation, or platform — can control, censor, or corrupt the RealityOS evidence layer.

🏛️

Federated Governance

Governance is distributed across a network of institutional nodes — universities, NGOs, independent media, and public institutions — each with weighted but bounded influence.

⚖️

Evidence-Weighted Voting

Dispute resolution is determined not by majority vote but by evidence weight, contributor track record, and structured argumentation — preventing mob rule on contested truths.

🔍

Transparent Moderation

All moderation actions are public, auditable, and reversible. Moderator decisions are themselves evidence objects — subject to community review and override.

🛡️

Anti-Capture Mechanisms

Structural controls prevent any single entity from dominating the network: node limits per organization, mandatory disclosure of institutional affiliations, and automatic conflict-of-interest flagging.

🌐

Open Standards Body

Core protocols are governed by an independent open standards body — not by a company or government — ensuring long-term interoperability and preventing proprietary lock-in.

🔄

Evolutionary Protocol

The system is designed to evolve: governance proposals are evidence objects themselves, debated and decided through the same transparent branching mechanisms applied to factual claims.
